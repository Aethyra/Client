Aethyra by Tametomo

An MMORPG client tuned for use with eAthena

Dependencies

Guichan 0.8.x, SDL, SDL_Image, SDL_Mixer, SDL_Net, SDL_TTF, Libxml2, Physfs 1.0.x, zlib 1.2.x, libcurl
